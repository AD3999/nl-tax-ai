# Market Landscape

> Competitive analysis and TaxWijs positioning.
> Updated: 2026-06-13

---

## Market Size

- **1.2 million ZZP workers** in the Netherlands (CBS 2025), growing ~8% per year
- **500,000+ Persian speakers** in the Netherlands, the vast majority with no native-language tax tool
- **20,000+ registered accountants** (RB/AA/RA) — target B2B buyers
- **4+ million IB returns** filed annually via Belastingdienst

**Serviceable addressable market (SAM) estimate:**
- ZZP segment: ~600,000 who self-manage their tax + ~200,000 who use an accountant but want supplemental guidance
- Expat segment: ~80,000 30%-ruling holders + ~150,000 other expats
- Accountant segment: ~5,000 small-to-mid firms who could benefit from an AI-enhanced workflow

## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors

| Product | Focus | Strengths | Weaknesses vs TaxWijs |
|---------|-------|-----------|----------------------|
| **Taxfix** | Consumer tax return filing (Germany focus, expanding) | Simple UX, mobile-first, e-filing | No NL-specific rules, no Persian, no accountant portal, not Dutch-first |
| **Tellow** | ZZP invoicing + bookkeeping | Strong ZZP brand, good BTW handling | Not an AI product, no conversational interface, no accountant collaboration |
| **Moneybird** | SME invoicing + bookkeeping | Clean design, good integrations | Tax filing guidance is very basic, no AI, no multilingual |
| **Aangifteprogramma Belastingdienst** | Official IB return tool | Free, official, always correct | Dutch only, no guidance, complex UI, no AI, no year-round value |
| **SnappTax** | ZZP tax return preparation | Dutch market, ZZP focus | Limited AI, no Persian, no accountant portal |

### Indirect Competitors

| Product | How it competes |
|---------|----------------|
| **Excel/Google Sheets** | Many ZZP workers self-calculate — we win by being faster and catching missed deductions |
| **Reddit/expatforum.nl** | Community Q&A — we win by being accurate, sourced, and trilingual |
| **WhatsApp with accountant** | Current communication pattern — we provide a structured alternative |
| **Generic ChatGPT/Claude** | Users ask AI tax questions — we win by having verified, sourced, year-specific rules |

## Why Now

1. **Wet DBA active enforcement (2025)** — ZZP workers need risk assessment tools now, not next year
2. **Startersaftrek sunset (2026 last year)** — urgency to claim before it disappears
3. **30% ruling phase-down (2024–2028)** — expats need guidance on their specific year
4. **Box 3 reform uncertainty** — complex situation creates demand for explanation
5. **AI is now good enough** — Claude claude-sonnet-4-6 can explain Dutch tax reliably when given the right context
6. **Persian community growth** — Iranian diaspora in NL is active, connected, and underserved

## Go-to-Market

### Phase 1 (Current): Organic / SEO
- Long-form trilingual content pages (`/zzp-tax-netherlands`, `/expat-tax-netherlands`)
- Tax calendar with ICS download as lead magnet
- Deduction checker as conversion tool (email gate before results)
- Reddit/expatforum presence

### Phase 2: Accountant Channel
- Accountant portal as the growth driver
- Each accountant brings their client list
- Marketplace listing for firm discovery

### Phase 3: Partnerships
- Belastingdienst awareness programs for non-Dutch speakers
- Expat community partnerships (InterNations, Expatica)
- Iranian business associations in the Netherlands
