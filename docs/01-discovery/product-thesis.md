# Product Thesis — TaxWijs

> Why TaxWijs exists, what it uniquely does, and the bet we are making.
> Updated: 2026-06-13

---

## The Problem

The Dutch tax system is one of the most complex in Europe. It has three income boxes, dozens of credits and deductions, five categories of toeslagen (benefits), special regimes for freelancers (ZZP), expats, and company directors (DGA), and deadlines that change each year.

**For ZZP workers** (1+ million in the Netherlands): Most do not understand the full picture of their own tax obligation. The ZVW health contribution is almost universally missed. Startersaftrek is claimed past eligibility or not claimed at all. Wet DBA risk is unknown to most. Missing deductions costs the average ZZP worker €1,500–€3,000 per year.

**For expats**: The 30% ruling is misapplied. Partial-year eligibility is almost never calculated correctly. Many overpay or underpay taxes in their first year. The language barrier (Dutch tax system explained only in Dutch) compounds the problem.

**For accountants**: The average Dutch accountant spends 40% of client engagement time on document collection, chasing missing information, and answering the same basic questions repeatedly. This is not advisory work — it is administrative overhead.

**For everyone**: Existing tools (Tellow, Moneybird, basic tax prep software) are either single-purpose or require significant tax knowledge to use. No single product handles the full journey from "I have income" to "my tax return is ready to file" in plain language across multiple languages.

---

## The TaxWijs Thesis

**TaxWijs is an AI Tax Operating System for Dutch taxpayers and accountants.**

It is not a chatbot. It is not a calculator. It is a complete tax preparation environment that:

1. **Knows the rules** — every Dutch tax rule for the current year, verified against official sources, updated annually
2. **Knows the user** — intake profile, uploaded documents, prior conversations, extracted data
3. **Explains everything** — in Dutch, English, and Persian, at the right level of detail
4. **Finds money** — proactively surfaces deductions, opportunities, and risks the user didn't know about
5. **Guides action** — from "I'm a ZZP worker with €72k revenue" to "here is your complete tax picture and what to do next"
6. **Connects professionals** — accountants and their clients work in one shared environment, not via email chains

## The Bet

We are betting that:

1. **Persian-speaking users in the Netherlands are massively underserved.** No tax product offers Persian as a first-class language. There are 500,000+ Persian speakers in the Netherlands, most of them ZZP workers or employees who navigate the tax system in a language they are not fluent in. A product that speaks their language earns their loyalty immediately.

2. **Accountants want a workspace, not more software.** Existing accountant tools are either back-office accounting systems or consumer apps. There is no modern SaaS workspace that puts the accountant at the center of a collaborative engagement with their client, powered by AI.

3. **Explanation is more valuable than automation.** Most tax software tries to hide the complexity. TaxWijs explains it. When the user understands *why* the ZVW applies, they trust the product and keep coming back.

4. **The compliance window is the conversion window.** People are most motivated to act on tax matters during the Q1/Q2 "aangifte season" and Q4 planning season. A product that is useful during those windows earns the right to be the year-round tax relationship.

---

## What TaxWijs Is NOT

- Not a registered tax advisor (see GAP-L06 — legal review required)
- Not an automated e-filing service (IB return guidance only — user still files)
- Not a bookkeeping or invoicing product (Tellow/Moneybird do that)
- Not a replacement for a human accountant in complex cases
- Not a general-purpose chatbot — it only answers Dutch tax questions

---

## The Core Promise

**"Know exactly where you stand on Dutch taxes — in any language, at any time."**

For a ZZP worker: "You owe €13,952 this year. Here is every deduction we found. Here is what you're missing. Here is what to do before 1 May."

For an accountant: "Your client's tax file is 82% ready. Here is what's missing. Here is what the AI found in the documents. Here is what to review."
